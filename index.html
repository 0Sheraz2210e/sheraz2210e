Welcome to alpine ascents.

Celebrating 37 years of leading successful expeditions in 2023, Alps Ascents International, Inc. is a leader in the climbing industry and has been instrumental in setting guiding standards in the international climbing community for most of our history. This year, we will offer more than 30 different expeditions and 50 training courses in 14 countries, including the Arctic and Antarctic regions. These expeditions range from three-day climbs of Mount Rainier to training courses in the North Cascades and Alaska, andascents of the highest mountains around the world. Alpine Ascents’ philosophy is based on a true love and understanding of mountain wilderness. Our mission and goals are simple: by using the best climbing talent in the country, we offer the opportunity for climbers at all levels to achieve their personal aspirations through quality, challenging, safe and fun expeditions. Unparalleled mountain adventures are what we do.

About Alpine ascents

Alpine ascents are the ultimate adventure for those seeking a challenge and a breathtaking view. At our website, we offer a range of alpine ascents to suit different skill levels and preferences. Whether you are a seasoned mountaineer or a beginner, we have the perfect ascent for you.

Our alpine ascents are led by experienced and certified guides who are passionate about mountaineering and committed to safety. They will guide you through the alpine landscape and provide you with the knowledge and skills you need to tackle even the toughest routes.

With our alpine ascents, you'll experience the beauty and grandeur of the mountains like never before. You'll see stunning glaciers, towering peaks, and vast expanses of alpine tundra. And with our small group sizes, you'll have a personalized experience that allows you to fully immerse yourself in the mountain environment.

At our website, we strive to make alpine ascents accessible to everyone. That's why we offer a range of ascents that cater to different budgets, schedules, and skill levels. So whether you're looking for a one-day ascent or a multi-day expedition, we have something for you.

So why wait? Join us for an alpine ascent today and experience the thrill and beauty of the mountains like never before.

Skills
Alpine climbing is a challenging activity that requires a high level of technical skills and physical fitness. Some of the key skills required for alpine ascents include rock and ice climbing, navigation, rope management, and glacier travel. Rock and ice climbing involve the use of specialized equipment such as crampons, ice axes, and ropes, and require knowledge of proper techniques for ascending and descending steep terrain. Navigation skills are critical for safely traversing the rugged alpine landscape, and include map reading, compass use, and GPS navigation. Rope management skills are important for properly securing oneself and teammates to prevent falls, while glacier travel skills involve understanding the dangers of crevasses and techniques for safely crossing them. Overall, alpine ascents demand a high level of proficiency in a range of technical skills, as well as physical stamina and mental fortitude to endure the demanding environment.
